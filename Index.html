STACKER


Instructions

The bar will go from left to right until you press the spacebar to stop the bar. You want to stack the bar as high as you can without missing the blocks you stacked from the bottom up. This will get smaller and faster as you go up. The goal is to get up to the blue bar for big prize. This game is so fun to play and will be unblocked in Mr. Mumm's app creations class. For you to start playing just click the start button.
